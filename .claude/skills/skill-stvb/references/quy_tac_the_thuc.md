# Quy tac the thuc van ban hanh chinh theo Phu luc I Nghi dinh 30/2020/ND-CP

## 1. Kho giay va le trang

- Kho giay A4 (210mm x 297mm), trinh bay doc theo chieu dai.
- Truong hop co bang, bieu co the trinh bay theo chieu ngang.
- Le tren: 20-25mm.
- Le duoi: 20-25mm.
- Le trai: 30-35mm.
- Le phai: 15-20mm.

## 2. Phong chu

- Phong chu: Times New Roman.
- Bo ma ky tu Unicode theo TCVN 6909:2001.
- Mau chu: den.

## 4. Khoang cach dong va doan

- Khoang cach giua cac dong: toi thieu dong don (single), toi da 1.5 lines.
- Khoang cach giua cac doan van: toi thieu 6pt.
- Quoc hieu va Tieu ngu cach nhau dong don.

## 5. Quy tac gach ngang

- Duoi Tieu ngu: duong ke ngang, net lien, dai bang do dai dong chu.
- Duoi ten co quan ban hanh: duong ke ngang, net lien.
- Duoi trich yeu (VB co ten loai): duong ke ngang dai 1/3 den 1/2 dong chu, dat can doi.
- KHONG dung underline de tao gach ngang; uu tien border-top trong code.

## 6. Bo cuc header (table 2 cot an vien)

- Cot trai: Ten CQ chu quan (khong dam) + Ten CQ ban hanh (dam) + gach ngang + So, ky hieu (+ trich yeu CV neu la cong van).
- Cot phai: Quoc hieu (dam) + Tieu ngu (dam) + gach ngang + Dia danh, ngay thang (nghieng).

## 7. Bo cuc footer (table 2 cot an vien)

- Cot trai (55%): Noi nhan (label 12pt nghieng dam, list 11pt).
- Cot phai (45%): Quyen han/chuc vu (dam) + "(Ky, ghi ro ho ten)" (nghieng) + Ho ten (dam).
- Nhan "Noi nhan:" ngang hang voi dong quyen han nguoi ky.

## 8. Cac truong hop ky

- TM. (Thay mat): nguoi dung dau ky truc tiep thay mat tap the.
- KT. (Ky thay): cap pho ky thay nguoi dung dau.
- Q. (Giao quyen): nguoi duoc giao quyen cap truong.
- TL. (Thua lenh): cap duoi ky theo lenh.
- TUQ. (Thua uy quyen): ky theo uy quyen.

## 9. Luu y ve ngay thang

- Viet day du ngay, thang, nam.
- Dung chu so A Rap.
- Ngay nho hon 10: them so 0 phia truoc (vd: ngay 05).
- Thang 1 va thang 2: them so 0 (vd: thang 01, thang 02).
