# Quy tac viet hoa trong van ban hanh chinh (Phu luc II Nghi dinh 30/2020/ND-CP)

## 1. Viet hoa vi phep dat cau

- Sau dau cham cau (.).
- Sau dau cham hoi (?).
- Sau dau cham than (!).
- Khi xuong dong.

## 2. Viet hoa danh tu rieng chi ten nguoi

### Ten nguoi Viet Nam
- Viet hoa chu cai dau cua tat ca cac am tiet.
- Vi du: Nguyen Van An, Tran Thi Binh.

### Ten hieu, ten goi nhan vat lich su
- Viet hoa chu cai dau cua tat ca cac am tiet.
- Vi du: Bac Ho, Anh Ca.

### Ten nguoi nuoc ngoai phien am sang tieng Viet
- Viet hoa chu cai dau cua am tiet dau tien trong moi thanh to.
- Vi du: Vla-di-mia Pu-tin, Ba-rac O-ba-ma.

## 3. Viet hoa ten dia ly

### Ten dia ly Viet Nam
- Viet hoa chu cai dau cua tat ca cac am tiet.
- Vi du: Ha Noi, Thanh pho Ho Chi Minh, Quang Ninh.

### Don vi hanh chinh kem ten rieng
- Viet hoa chu cai dau cua ten don vi hanh chinh khi di lien voi ten rieng.
- Vi du: tinh Quang Tri, huyen Hai Lang, xa An Ninh.

### Ten dia ly nuoc ngoai phien am
- Viet hoa chu cai dau cua am tiet dau tien.
- Vi du: Luan Don, Pa-ri.

## 4. Viet hoa ten co quan, to chuc

### Co quan, to chuc cua Viet Nam
- Viet hoa chu cai dau cua ten co quan, to chuc va cac tu chi loai hinh.
- Vi du: Bo Noi vu, Uy ban nhan dan tinh Quang Tri, Hoi dong nhan dan xa An Ninh.

### Viet tat ten co quan
- Dung chu viet tat theo quy dinh: UBND, HDND, CP, BNV...

## 5. Viet hoa cac truong hop khac

### Danh tu dac biet
- Nhan dan, Nha nuoc (khi dung trong nhan xung, doc lap, the hien su tran trong).

### Ten huan chuong, huy chuong, danh hieu
- Viet hoa chu cai dau cua cac am tiet co y nghia phan biet.
- Vi du: Huan chuong Sao vang, Anh hung Lao dong.

### Chuc vu, hoc vi, danh hieu
- Viet hoa ten chuc vu, hoc vi neu di kem ten co quan hoac dung trong van ban hanh chinh.
- Vi du: Chu tich Uy ban nhan dan, Thu tuong Chinh phu.

### Danh tu chung da rieng hoa
- Tu hoac cum tu chi ten goi trong truong hop dung nhan xung, doc lap, the hien su tran trong.
- Vi du: Dang (chi Dang Cong san Viet Nam), Bac (chi Chu tich Ho Chi Minh).

### Ten cac ngay le, ngay ky niem
- Viet hoa chu cai dau cua am tiet dau tien va ten rieng.
- Vi du: ngay Quoc khanh, Tet Nguyen dan.

### Ten loai van ban khi vien dan
- Viet hoa chu cai dau cua ten loai van ban.
- Vi du: theo Khoan 2 Dieu 5 Nghi dinh so 30/2020/ND-CP.

### Ten su kien lich su va trieu dai
- Viet hoa chu cai dau cua am tiet dau tien va ten rieng.
- Vi du: Cach mang thang Tam, trieu Nguyen.

## 6. Luu y khi soan van ban

- Phai nhat quan cach viet hoa trong toan bo van ban.
- Ten co quan phai viet dung ten chinh thuc, day du.
- Khi vien dan van ban lan dau: ghi day du ten loai, so, ky hieu, thoi gian ban hanh, ten co quan, va trich yeu.
- Khi vien dan tiep theo: chi ghi ten loai va so, ky hieu.
