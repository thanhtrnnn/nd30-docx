# Ap dung cho boi canh chinh quyen dia phuong 2 cap

## Muc tieu

Khi soan van ban cho co quan cap xa/phuong trong boi canh chinh quyen 2 cap, can dung cap tham quyen va can cu.

## Checklist truoc khi ky

1. Dung ten co quan ban hanh, cap quan ly va tham quyen ky.
2. Dung cap ky: TM/KT/TL/TUQ phu hop voi phan quyen noi bo.
3. Can cu co toi thieu:
   - 1 van ban ve to chuc bo may/chinh quyen dia phuong 2 cap.
   - 1 van ban ve linh vuc chuyen mon cua noi dung soan thao.
   - 1 van ban trien khai cua tinh (neu co).
4. Noi nhan phai bao gom co quan cap tren va bo phan thi hanh truc tiep.

## Nhom van ban goi y can tra cuu

- Nghi quyet/ket luan/cong dien lien quan van hanh chinh quyen dia phuong 2 cap.
- Van ban phan cap, phan quyen, phan dinh tham quyen.
- Ke hoach trien khai cua UBND tinh phu hop dia ban.

## Luu y

Neu can cu khong ro hieu luc hoac khong tim duoc van ban dia phuong phu hop, phai danh dau `CAN_XAC_MINH_HIEU_LUC` truoc khi phat hanh ban chinh thuc.
