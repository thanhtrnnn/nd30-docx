# Phan quyen ky van ban hanh chinh (theo ND30/2020)

## Cac truong hop ky

### 1. TM. (Thay mat)
- Nguoi dung dau ky truc tiep thay mat tap the lanh dao hoac ten co quan, to chuc.
- Ghi: `TM.` truoc ten tap the hoac ten co quan.
- Vi du: `TM. UY BAN NHAN DAN` / `TM. BAN CHAP HANH DANG BO`.

### 2. KT. (Ky thay)
- Cap pho ky thay nguoi dung dau co quan, to chuc.
- Truong hop cap pho duoc giao phu trach hoac dieu hanh thi thuc hien ky nhu cap pho ky thay cap truong.
- Ghi: `KT.` truoc chuc vu cua nguoi dung dau.
- Vi du: `KT. CHU TICH` / `PHO CHU TICH`.

### 3. Q. (Giao quyen)
- Nguoi duoc giao quyen cap truong.
- Ghi: `Q.` truoc chuc vu cua nguoi dung dau co quan, to chuc.
- Vi du: `Q. CHU TICH`.

### 4. TL. (Thua lenh)
- Cap duoi ky theo lenh cua nguoi dung dau co quan, to chuc.
- Ghi: `TL.` truoc chuc vu cua nguoi dung dau.
- Vi du: `TL. CHU TICH` / `TRUONG PHONG NOI VU`.

### 5. TUQ. (Thua uy quyen)
- Nguoi duoc uy quyen ky van ban trong pham vi duoc uy quyen.
- Phai co van ban uy quyen hop phap.
- Ghi: `TUQ.` truoc chuc vu cua nguoi dung dau.
- Vi du: `TUQ. CHU TICH` / `TRUONG PHONG TAI CHINH`.

## Trinh bay khoi ky

### Dong quyen han (neu co)
- Chu IN HOA, dam.
- Vi du: `TM. UY BAN NHAN DAN`, `KT. CHU TICH`.

### Dong chuc vu ky
- Chu IN HOA, dam.
- Vi du: `CHU TICH`, `PHO CHU TICH`.

### Dong "(Ky, ghi ro ho ten)"
- Chu nghieng, co chu 13-14.

### Ho ten nguoi ky
- Chu dam, co chu 13-14.
- KHONG ghi hoc ham, hoc vi truoc ho ten (tru don vi vu trang nhan dan, giao duc, y te, khoa hoc theo quy dinh rieng).

## Vi tri khoi ky

- Khoi ky dat ben PHAI van ban, ngang hang voi nhan "Noi nhan:" ben trai.
- Giua dong chuc vu va ho ten nguoi ky phai co khoang trong de ky (khoang 3-4 dong trong).

## Luu y

- Khong ghi nhung chuc vu ma Nha nuoc khong quy dinh.
- Chuc vu ghi tren van ban la chuc vu lanh dao chinh thuc trong co quan, to chuc.
