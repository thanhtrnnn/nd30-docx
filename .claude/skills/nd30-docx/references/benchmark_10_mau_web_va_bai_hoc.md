## Mau hinh bo cuc rut ra tu doi chieu

### Doi voi QUYET DINH

1. Sau dong tieu de `QUYET DINH` va trich yeu in hoa, thuong co dong xac dinh chu the ban hanh (vi du: `UY BAN NHAN DAN ...`).
2. Khoi `Can cu` sap theo cap van ban tu luat -> nghi dinh -> thong tu -> van ban dia phuong/chuyen nganh.
3. Neu co van ban sua doi, cau truc viet day du: `... duoc sua doi, bo sung boi ...`.
4. Truoc phan dieu khoan thuong co dong `Theo de nghi cua ... tai To trinh so ...`.
5. Mau dieu khoan pho bien:
- Dieu 1: Ban hanh kem theo noi dung/quy che/quy dinh.
- Dieu 2: Hieu luc thi hanh + van ban het hieu luc/thay the.
- Dieu 3: Trach nhiem thi hanh (liet ke ro co quan, don vi, ca nhan).
6. Khoi ky can dung dung tham quyen (`TM`, `KT`) va khop nguoi ky/chuc vu.

### Doi voi KE HOACH

1. Cau truc muc lon on dinh theo huong dan hanh chinh:
- I. Muc dich, yeu cau
- II. Nhiem vu, giai phap
- III. Kinh phi/nguon luc
- IV. To chuc thuc hien
2. Nhiem vu thuong co ket qua dau ra ro, moc thoi gian, don vi chu tri, don vi phoi hop.
3. Co cac dong che do bao cao va to chuc kiem tra/giam sat.
4. Van ban ke hoach chat luong cao thuong co phu luc bang tien do de de theo doi.

## Khoang cach giua mau sinh va mau web

1. Mau sinh da dat bo cuc co ban ND30, nhung can nang cap tinh quy pham cho Quyet dinh:
- Can bo sung dong chu the ban hanh truoc khoi can cu.
- Chuan hoa cau truc Dieu 2 (hieu luc + thay the/huy bo neu co).
2. Mau Ke hoach can lam ro hon tinh kha thi:
- Tang thong tin KPI/dau ra theo nhiem vu.
- Uu tien bo sung phu luc tien do (moc thoi gian, co quan, san pham).
3. Chuoi can cu can chuan hoa sau hon:
- Neu dan chieu van ban da sua doi, bat buoc viet ro van ban sua doi.

## Rule bo sung vao skill (ap dung ngay)

1. Quy trinh sinh QUYET DINH phai check bat buoc 6 diem:
- Co dong chu the ban hanh;
- Co dong `Theo de nghi...`;
- Dieu 1 noi dung chinh;
- Dieu 2 hieu luc + thay the/huy bo (neu co);
- Dieu 3 trach nhiem thi hanh;
- Khoi ky dung tham quyen.
2. Quy trinh sinh KE HOACH phai check bat buoc 4 diem:
- Du 4 muc I-II-III-IV;
- Moi nhiem vu co thoi han + don vi chu tri;
- Co che do bao cao;
- Co phu luc tien do (neu la ke hoach nam/chuyen de lon).
3. Neu nguon web chuyen PDF khong trich duoc, bat buoc ghi chu trong `ghi_chu_tra_cuu`:
- URL
- ngay tra cuu
- trang thai: `CAN_XAC_MINH_NOI_DUNG_GOC`

## Khuyen nghi nang cap tiep theo

1. Bo sung script tao phu luc bang tien do tu JSON (`phu_luc_tien_do`).
2. Bo sung validator truoc khi xuat docx de chan file thieu Dieu 2/Dieu 3 (quyet dinh) hoac thieu muc III/IV (ke hoach).
3. Bo sung danh sach mau cau van phong cach phap ly (thu vien cau cho can cu, hieu luc, trach nhiem thi hanh).
