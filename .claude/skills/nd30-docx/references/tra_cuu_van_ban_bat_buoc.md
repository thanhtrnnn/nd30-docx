# Tra cuu van ban bat buoc truoc khi soan

## Muc tieu

Dam bao moi van ban duoc tao ra deu co can cu phap ly duoc tra cuu va doi chieu.

## Quy trinh 5 buoc

1. Xac dinh loai van ban: cong van / quyet dinh / ke hoach.
2. Xac dinh chu de nghiep vu: can bo, tai chinh, chuyen doi so, dat dai...
3. Tra cuu van ban cap Trung uong (luat, nghi dinh, thong tu) lien quan truc tiep.
4. Tra cuu van ban cap dia phuong (nghi quyet, ke hoach, quyet dinh UBND tinh) neu co.
5. Kiem tra tinh trang hieu luc va ngay ban hanh, ghi vao `ghi_chu_tra_cuu`.

## Tieu chi dat

- Toi thieu 2 can cu phap ly cho van ban thong thuong.
- Toi thieu 3 can cu phap ly cho van ban co yeu to to chuc bo may/phan cap tham quyen.
- Co ghi ro nguon tra cuu + ngay tra cuu.
