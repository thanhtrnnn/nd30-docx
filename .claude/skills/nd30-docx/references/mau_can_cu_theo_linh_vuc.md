# Bo mau can cu theo tung loai van ban

Muc dich: cung cap bo can cu mau de agent lap nhanh truoc khi soan van ban.
Luu y: cac can cu ben duoi la mau goi y, phai tra cuu lai hieu luc tai thoi diem soan.

## 1) Linh vuc can bo

### Can cu loi (goi y)
- Luat To chuc chinh quyen dia phuong (phien ban dang hieu luc).
- Luat Can bo, cong chuc (va van ban sua doi, bo sung neu co).
- Nghi dinh 30/2020/ND-CP ve cong tac van thu.
- Van ban phan cap/phan quyen cua UBND tinh lien quan cong tac can bo cap xa.

### Mau can_cu
- "Can cu Luat To chuc chinh quyen dia phuong ..."
- "Can cu Luat Can bo, cong chuc ..."
- "Can cu Nghi dinh 30/2020/ND-CP ngay 05/3/2020 cua Chinh phu ve cong tac van thu"
- "Can cu [Quyet dinh/Ke hoach] cua UBND [tinh] ve cong tac can bo ..."

## 2) Linh vuc chuyen doi so

### Can cu loi (goi y)
- Luat Chuyen doi so 2025 (neu ap dung theo thoi diem).
- Nghi dinh 30/2020/ND-CP (the thuc van ban).
- Quyet dinh/Ke hoach chuyen doi so cap tinh/cap xa.
- Van ban huong dan trien khai chinh quyen dia phuong 2 cap co lien quan chuyen doi so.

### Mau can_cu
- "Can cu Luat Chuyen doi so ..."
- "Can cu Nghi dinh 30/2020/ND-CP ..."
- "Can cu Ke hoach .../KH-UBND cua UBND [tinh] ve chuyen doi so ..."
- "Can cu van ban chi dao cua cap co tham quyen ve van hanh chinh quyen dia phuong 2 cap ..."

## 3) Linh vuc tai chinh

### Can cu loi (goi y)
- Luat Ngan sach nha nuoc.
- Van ban huong dan thi hanh Luat Ngan sach nha nuoc (nghi dinh/thong tu).
- Nghi dinh 30/2020/ND-CP.
- Nghi quyet HDND/Quyet dinh UBND ve dinh muc, phan bo, du toan tai dia phuong.

### Mau can_cu
- "Can cu Luat Ngan sach nha nuoc ..."
- "Can cu [Nghi dinh/Thong tu] huong dan lap, chap hanh va quyet toan ngan sach ..."
- "Can cu Nghi dinh 30/2020/ND-CP ..."
- "Can cu Nghi quyet HDND/Quyet dinh UBND [tinh] ve phan bo du toan nam ..."

## 4) Linh vuc dat dai

### Can cu loi (goi y)
- Luat Dat dai 2024 (hoac ban dang hieu luc tai thoi diem soan).
- Van ban huong dan thi hanh Luat Dat dai (nghi dinh/thong tu).
- Nghi dinh 30/2020/ND-CP.
- Quyet dinh/Quy che pho hop cua UBND tinh ve dat dai voi cap xa.

### Mau can_cu
- "Can cu Luat Dat dai ..."
- "Can cu [Nghi dinh/Thong tu] huong dan thi hanh Luat Dat dai ..."
- "Can cu Nghi dinh 30/2020/ND-CP ..."
- "Can cu Quyet dinh .../QĐ-UBND cua UBND [tinh] ban hanh quy che pho hop trong linh vuc dat dai voi UBND cap xa ..."
