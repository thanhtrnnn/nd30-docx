# Huong dan bo cuc noi dung va cach trinh bay

Tai lieu nay quy dinh cach viet de van ban day du, ro rang va de thi hanh.

## A. Doi voi Quyet dinh

### 1) Bo cuc bat buoc
- Ten loai "QUYET DINH" (IN HOA, dam, canh giua).
- Trich yeu noi dung (dam, canh giua, co gach ngang phia duoi).
- Dong chu the ban hanh (vd: "UY BAN NHAN DAN XA AN NINH") - IN HOA, dam, canh giua.
- Phan can cu: 3-5 can cu, theo thu tu tu van ban cap cao den van ban trien khai (nghieng).
- Dong "Theo de nghi cua ... tai To trinh so ..." (nghieng, truoc dong "QUYET DINH:").
- Dong "QUYET DINH:" (IN HOA, dam, canh giua).
- It nhat 3 dieu:
  - Dieu 1: Noi dung quyet dinh/chu the duoc giao.
  - Dieu 2: Hieu luc thi hanh + van ban het hieu luc/thay the (neu co).
  - Dieu 3: Trach nhiem thi hanh (liet ke ro co quan, don vi, ca nhan).
- Noi nhan (ben trai) va khoi ky (ben phai) trong table 2 cot.

### 2) Cach viet
- Cau ngan, ro chu the - hanh dong - pham vi.
- Tranh cau mo ho: "thuc hien nghiem tuc" ma khong co doi tuong cu the.
- Dung dong tu phap ly phu hop: "giao", "phan cong", "ban hanh", "co hieu luc".

### 3) Kiem tra chat luong
- Co xac dinh ro doi tuong thi hanh khong?
- Co moc thoi gian hieu luc khong?
- Co noi nhan dung cap va du thanh phan khong?

## B. Doi voi Ke hoach

### 1) Bo cuc bat buoc
- I. Can cu/Muc dich, yeu cau.
- II. Muc tieu (chi tieu dinh luong neu co the).
- III. Nhiem vu, giai phap.
- IV. To chuc thuc hien (phan cong ro bo phan chu tri - phoi hop).
- V. Che do bao cao/kinh phi (tuy tinh chat van ban).

### 2) Cach viet
- Moi muc nen co dau dong danh so 1,2,3.
- Muc tieu nen gan voi ket qua do luong duoc.
- Giai phap nen gan voi nguon luc va thoi han.

### 3) Kiem tra chat luong
- Ke hoach co chi ro ai lam, lam gi, khi nao khong?
- Co tieu chi danh gia ket qua khong?
- Co bo tri kinh phi/nguon luc neu can khong?

## C. Quy tac trinh bay chung theo ND30

- Font Times New Roman, co chu 13-14pt, mau den.
- Le trang: tren/duoi 20-25mm, trai 30-35mm, phai 15-20mm.
- Noi dung canh deu 2 le, lui dau dong 1cm/1.27cm.
- Cac phan tieu de/chuong muc ro rang.
- Dau cau day du, thong nhat cach viet hoa theo Phu luc II ND30.
- Khoi ky phu hop TM/KT/Q/TL/TUQ.
- Noi nhan (trai) ngang hang khoi ky (phai) trong table 2 cot.
- Khong phat hanh ban chinh thuc neu con co "CAN_XAC_MINH_HIEU_LUC".
- Tham chieu: `references/quy_tac_the_thuc.md` va `references/quy_tac_viet_hoa.md`.
